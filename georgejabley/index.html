However long makes sense for you — there's no lock-in contract. Some clients need a few hours a week ongoing, others need a short burst to clear a backlog and then step back. We agree on scope and cadence upfront, and it can flex as your needs change.

That's exactly what the free audit above is for — no pressure, no pitch. We'll look at where your time is actually going and you'll walk away with a clear picture either way, even if the answer is "not yet."
